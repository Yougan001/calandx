ABOUT US

Company Profile

Founded in the core of Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area - Guangzhou Nansha, Calandx is an expert in smart thermal management solutions using thin-film technology.

Our R&D and production base (Nansha Hengli Biomedical Industrial Park) has composite material production lines and thin-film production lines. We specialize in nano-conductive filling masterbatch and thin-film products.

With deep expertise in nano-technology, we deliver smart thermal management solutions to home appliances, outdoor equipment, medical devices, and industrial applications, empowering industry upgrades.
